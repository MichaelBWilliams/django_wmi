About

The population atlas plots high resolution population density estimates provided from WorldPop.org with MFI branch locations. This dataset provides MFI staff with an overview of how the population in their country is organzied spatially. It is meant to assist in planning and operations tasks.

Warning! Entire Atlas files are very large (>15mb). For smaller download size, administrative units can be downloaded individually below.
